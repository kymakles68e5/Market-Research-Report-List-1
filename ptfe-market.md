<p><p>The PTFE market encompasses various forms, each suited for specific applications. Granular PTFE is ideal for molding applications due to its bulk properties, while fine powder is used in coatings and lubricants for its small particle size. Dispersion PTFE finds use in paints and coatings, offering excellent adhesion and durability. Micronized PTFE, with its ultra-fine particles, enhances properties like slip and non-stick characteristics, making it suitable for high-performance applications like gaskets and seals. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
