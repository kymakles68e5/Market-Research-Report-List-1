<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multi-mode Couplers</li><li>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic couplers market is categorized into three main types: Single Mode Couplers, Multi-mode Couplers, and Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers. Single Mode Couplers are designed for a single light path, enhancing long-distance communication with minimal loss. Multi-mode Couplers support multiple light paths, suitable for short-range applications. Polarization Maintaining Couplers preserve the light's polarization state, vital for certain applications in telecommunications and sensing. Each type addresses specific needs and performance requirements in fiber optic systems.</p></p>
