<p><p>Ethylene-Propylene-Diene Monomer (EPDM) is a synthetic rubber widely used for its excellent resistance to heat, oxidation, and aging. The market for EPDM can be segmented based on polymerization methods: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent which allows for better control over molecular weight and structure, resulting in superior performance characteristics. In contrast, suspension polymerization produces larger rubber particles dispersed in water, leading to lower production costs and easy processing. Both methods cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
