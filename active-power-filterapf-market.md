<p><strong>The Active Power Filter(APF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Power Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Power Filters (APF) are devices used in electrical systems to enhance power quality by reducing harmonic distortions, voltage fluctuations, and reactive power. The market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Shunt Active Power Filters** divert harmonics away from the power supply by injecting compensating currents. </p><p>2. **Series Active Power Filters** mitigate voltage-related issues by injecting or absorbing voltage components. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Active Power Filters** combine both shunt and series technologies, offering versatile solutions for complex power quality challenges.</p></p>
