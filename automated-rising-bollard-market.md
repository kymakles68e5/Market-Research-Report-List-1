<p><p>The automated rising bollard market can be classified into three main types: hydraulic powered, pneumatic powered, and electric powered systems. Hydraulic powered bollards use fluid pressure to raise and lower, offering strength and durability for heavy-duty security. Pneumatic powered bollards operate using compressed air, providing quick response times and ease of installation. Electric powered bollards utilize electric motors for operation, allowing for precise control and integration with smart technologies. Each type serves specific security needs in urban environments.</p></p>
