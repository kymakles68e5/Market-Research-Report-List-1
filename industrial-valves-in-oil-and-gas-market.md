<p><p>Industrial valves are crucial in the oil and gas market for controlling fluid flow and ensuring safety. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with a spherical disc, while plug valves use a cylindrical plug for flow regulation. Gate valves provide on/off control with a sliding gate, and globe valves allow precise flow adjustment through a movable disk. Butterfly valves feature a rotating disc for efficient throttling. Each type serves specific applications in pipelines and processing systems, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
