<p><p>The Multi GNSS Receiver Chips market is primarily segmented into single frequency and dual frequency types. Single frequency chips operate on a single frequency band, making them cost-effective and suitable for basic positioning applications. In contrast, dual frequency chips utilize two frequency bands, enhancing accuracy and reliability by mitigating errors such as ionospheric delays. This makes them ideal for advanced applications like high-precision agriculture, surveying, and autonomous vehicles, driving demand in sectors requiring improved performance and robustness in GNSS solutions.</p></p>
