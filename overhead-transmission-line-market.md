<p><p>The overhead transmission line market encompasses various types, including Optical Fiber Composite Overhead Ground Wire (OPGW) and Steel Strand conductors. OPGW integrates optical fibers within the ground wire, facilitating telecommunications and data transmission alongside power delivery. This dual functionality enhances grid reliability and efficiency. Steel Strand conductors, on the other hand, are primarily focused on providing mechanical strength and support for overhead power lines, ensuring durability against environmental stressors. Both types are essential for modern energy infrastructure.</p></p>
