The 0-10V drivers allow for ambient light dimming by adjusting the voltage, suitable for various environments. CLK drivers use clock signals for precise timing control, ideal for synchronized lighting applications. PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) drivers control brightness by varying the duration of on/off cycles, providing efficient energy use and heat management. Each type serves specific needs within hazardous locations, ensuring safety and functionality.</p></p>
